Residential

Smart Living for the Modern Villa

A whole-home ecosystem that moves at the pace of luxury living.

The Zakey Smart Villa is a turnkey deployment of central control, lighting, climate, security, and energy intelligence designed for villas, signature residences, and architect-led projects where the experience must feel effortless from the first moment.

Get a Quote Explore Products

The challenge

Why villas need more than a smart speaker

  1. Coordinating climate across many rooms

    Villas combine multiple HVAC zones that each need independent scheduling. Without a unified system, residents adjust each thermostat manually — and zones often fight each other.

  2. Fragmented lighting scenes

    Premium villas deserve lighting that responds to occasion, time of day, and occupancy — not individual switches scattered across rooms that behave differently.

  3. Security gaps at the perimeter and inside

    Large properties have many entry points. Monitoring them in real time, alerting the owner, and locking down automatically after hours requires a single integrated layer, not separate apps.

  4. Energy waste that scales with size

    A villa's energy footprint is substantial. Without intelligent monitoring and automated cutoffs, lighting, HVAC, and appliances run long after they are needed.

  5. Premium finish standards with hidden technology

    Architects and interior designers specify hardware that complements — not interrupts — the design language. Visible conduits, clunky panels, and mismatched switches undermine a premium build.

The Zakey answer

One ecosystem, every room

Zakey ties every subsystem into one orchestrated ecosystem through the Mesh Bridge — a central hub that coordinates the Aura Panel, Sense Engine sensors, Climate Hub, and security layer without requiring the homeowner to think about which app to open. Set a "Welcome Home" scene once; Zakey handles the rest every time you arrive.

The Aura Panel on every floor gives residents tactile, elegant control while the Zakey App provides the same reach from anywhere in the world. Curtains lower, lights soften, HVAC pre-cools the master suite, and the perimeter locks — all triggered by a single departing fingerprint or a scheduled scene. The result is a villa that feels alive, anticipatory, and effortlessly premium.

What's inside

Every subsystem, ready out of the box

  • Central Control

    The Zakey Mesh Bridge orchestrates all subsystems from a single hub. The Aura Panel at each floor gives residents tactile, intuitive command over every connected device.

  • Lighting

    Scene-based lighting across every room — from bright task light in the kitchen to a 5% ambient glow in the cinema — all dimmable, schedulable, and tied to presence sensors.

  • Curtains & Shading

    Motorised curtains and blackout blinds respond to scenes, schedules, and sun-intensity sensors — protecting privacy and furnishings while maximising natural light on demand.

  • Climate

    The Climate Hub manages every HVAC zone independently, using occupancy data from the Sense Engine to heat or cool rooms only when needed — down to 0.5 °C precision.

  • Security

    Perimeter cameras, smart door locks, and motion alerts work together. The "Away" scene arms the full perimeter; the app shows live status and sends instant alerts for any anomaly.

  • Sensors

    Zakey's Sense Engine places motion, door-contact, temperature, humidity, and air-quality sensors throughout the villa, feeding real-time data to every automation decision.

  • Energy Monitoring

    Real-time energy dashboards show consumption per room and per device. Automated cutoffs for unoccupied zones and appliance scheduling reduce a large villa's bill by 20–35%.

  • App & Voice

    The Zakey App puts full villa control in your pocket — on-premises or remotely. Works with Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri so family members can use the voice assistant they already know.

Day in the life

Scenes that move with you

  • Welcome Home

    When the front door unlocks after 17:00 on a weekday

    Then Zakey raises the entryway lights to 70 %, fades on the kitchen ambient strip, pre-cools the master suite to 22 °C, and opens the living-room curtains to the sunset position

  • Goodnight

    When the "Goodnight" scene is activated from the master-bedroom Aura Panel

    Then Zakey dims all lights to 0 % across every floor, closes all curtains and blinds, locks every entry point, and drops the HVAC to sleep-comfort mode at 19 °C

  • Movie Night

    When the cinema scene is triggered from the app or the living-room panel

    Then Zakey dims the cinema to 5 % bias lighting, lowers the blackout curtains, sets the HVAC to a quieter fan speed, and disables notifications on in-room speakers
