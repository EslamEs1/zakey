Software Platform

Control Every Space from One Intelligent Experience

Seven software pillars unify the resident app, control panels, automation scenes, voice integration, business dashboard, energy insights, and guest experience.

Request a Demo Become a Partner

Pillar 01

Zakey Mobile App

Your home in your pocket — full control, anywhere you are.

The Zakey iOS and Android app gives residents a single, beautifully organised interface for their entire home. Browse every room, adjust individual devices, or fire a scene with a single tap. Build schedules that match your daily rhythm — wake up to warm light, arrive home to the perfect temperature — and receive instant push notifications when doors open, sensors trigger, or automations run. For those managing multiple properties, the app handles all of them from one account without switching apps or profiles.

  • Live device status across every room
  • One-tap scene activation
  • Visual schedule builder with repeat rules
  • Push notifications for devices and automations
  • Multi-property support under a single account
Request a Demo →

Pillar 02

Zakey Control Panels

Every room deserves a command centre — not a phone hunt.

Zakey wall panels bring the entire room's intelligence to your fingertips the moment you enter. Customise each panel with the scene tiles that matter most in that space — Morning, Focus, Cinema, Relax — and switch between them with a single press. Ambient display mode shows the time, temperature, and active scene when the panel is at rest, turning a functional device into an elegant fixture. Panels install in a standard wall box and connect to the Zakey hub without running new cable, and they operate locally so scenes fire even when your internet is temporarily unavailable.

  • Customisable scene tiles per room
  • Ambient display mode when idle
  • Master controls for all devices in the space
  • Plug-and-play installation in standard wall boxes
  • Local processing — works offline
View Products →

Pillar 03

Smart Scenes & Automation

Set the conditions once — your home handles the rest.

The Zakey scene builder uses a visual, drag-and-drop canvas to connect triggers with device actions across your entire home. Choose from time-based triggers, sensor readings, sunrise and sunset offsets, geofencing events, or presence detection — then chain any combination of lights, climate, curtains, and audio into a single, coordinated response. Pre-built templates get you started in seconds; advanced users can nest scenes inside scenes to build layered routines as sophisticated as the space demands. Every automation runs locally for instant response, with cloud sync for remote access.

  • Drag-and-drop visual scene builder
  • Sensor-triggered automations (motion, door, temperature)
  • Time, sunrise/sunset, and geofencing triggers
  • Nested scenes for layered routines
  • Pre-built templates for common living patterns
Explore Smart Villa →

Pillar 04

Voice Assistant Integration

Speak naturally — your home listens across every major platform.

Zakey works with Alexa and is compatible with Google Assistant, giving residents hands-free control over every device and scene without lifting a finger. Siri Shortcuts support brings the same convenience to the Apple ecosystem, letting you launch scenes from the Shortcuts app or with a custom phrase. Ask to dim the living room, activate the Good Night scene, or check whether the front door is locked — and the right thing happens immediately. Voice commands reach the same device graph as the app, so everything stays in sync across all control surfaces.

  • Voice-activated scene activation
  • Natural language device control by room or name
  • Works with Alexa for hands-free automation
  • Compatible with Google Assistant
  • Siri Shortcuts support for Apple users

Pillar 05

Business / Project Dashboard

One web portal for every property in your portfolio.

The Zakey operator dashboard is a web-based management layer built for system integrators, hotel operations teams, and real-estate developers overseeing multiple installations simultaneously. See every property on a single screen — KPI tiles, building schematics, and live device status at a glance. Role-based access control ensures that hotel managers see only their building, while a head-office team sees the full portfolio. Run remote diagnostics without sending a technician on-site, pull usage analytics into your own reporting tools via CSV export, and configure alert routing so the right team member is notified the moment something needs attention.

  • Multi-property overview on a single dashboard
  • Role-based user management and access control
  • Remote device diagnostics without site visits
  • Usage analytics with CSV export
  • Configurable alert and notification centre

Pillar 06

Energy & Device Insights

Know what your building is consuming — and where you can save.

Zakey tracks energy consumption at the device, room, and building level in real time, giving operators the granular visibility they need to reduce waste and plan maintenance before failures occur. Live consumption graphs surface unexpected spikes the moment they happen, while daily and monthly trend charts reveal the patterns behind your utility bills. Device uptime monitoring flags equipment behaving outside normal parameters, and the anomaly alert engine sends a notification before a failing device disrupts the resident experience. Scheduled efficiency reports land in your inbox every week with prioritised cost-saving recommendations.

  • Real-time consumption graphs by device, room, and building
  • Device uptime monitoring with health indicators
  • Anomaly alerts for unexpected energy spikes or failures
  • Scheduled efficiency reports delivered by email
  • Actionable cost-saving recommendations

Pillar 07

Guest / Hotel Room Experience

A stay that feels personal — without burdening the guest with technology.

The Zakey guest experience layer elevates every hotel room stay into a personalised moment. A branded welcome screen greets each guest by name when they enter, and a curated set of scene tiles — Relax, Sleep, Work, Bright — lets them shape the room's atmosphere with a single press. Do-not-disturb mode activates from the in-room panel without touching the phone, and the check-out flow automatically resets every device to the hotel's default state when the guest departs. Guests who prefer to use their own phone can scan a QR code to access room controls instantly — no app download required.

  • Personalised welcome screen with guest name
  • In-room scene tiles: Relax, Sleep, Work, Bright
  • Do-not-disturb activation from the room panel
  • Check-out flow resets the room to hotel defaults automatically
  • No app download required — QR code access from any phone
