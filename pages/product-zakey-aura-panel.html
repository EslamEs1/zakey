Zakey Aura Panel

Your home's intelligence, at your fingertips.

A 10-inch capacitive glass surface that serves as the central command for your Zakey ecosystem. Control scenes, dim lights, adjust the thermostat, view camera feeds, and trigger automations — all from a single, beautifully crafted wall panel.

Request a Quote Download Specs

What the Aura Panel is

The Aura Panel is Zakey's flagship in-wall controller — a flush-mounted, 10-inch capacitive glass display that unifies every subsystem in your home into a single, responsive interface. From adjusting the colour temperature of your living room lighting to viewing a live camera feed or launching a multi-device scene, the Aura Panel handles it with a single touch. Its slim aluminium chassis sits flush with any wall surface, making it as much a design statement as a control device.

Deployed in living rooms, master bedroom walls, hotel reception desks, executive office lobbies, and home-cinema suites, the Aura Panel is the natural centrepiece of any Zakey installation. It pairs with the Zakey Mesh Bridge for whole-home reach, integrates natively with the Zakey Sense Engine for AI-driven scene suggestions, and bridges third-party Matter, Zigbee 3.0, and Wi-Fi 5 devices so every piece of hardware in your space responds to a single source of truth.

Scenarios it powers

  • Morning routine

    When 07:00 arrives on a weekday

    Then Aura Panel raises bedroom blinds, sets lights to 40% warm white, and starts the climate at 22°C

  • Movie night

    When the 'Cinema' scene is tapped on the Aura Panel

    Then Aura Panel dims all lights to 5%, lowers the projector screen, closes curtains, and silences all alerts

  • Goodnight

    When the 'Goodnight' scene is activated from the Aura Panel

    Then Aura Panel locks all doors, arms the perimeter sensors, turns off all lights, and sets the climate to sleep mode at 19°C
