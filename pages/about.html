About Zakey

Designing Smarter Spaces with Zakey

Zakey crafts premium smart-living systems for villas, hotels, offices, and developments — combining elegant hardware, intuitive software, and reliable, project-ready support.

Talk to Us See Our Projects

Our Story

Zakey was founded by a team of engineers and hospitality professionals who had spent years watching smart-home technology promise everything and deliver frustration. The setups were complicated, the interfaces were ugly, and the support — when projects went live — was almost nonexistent. The smartest spaces were often the ones with the most problems.

We started from a different premise: that a truly intelligent space should feel effortless. Not effortless in the sense of "press one button and hope," but effortless in the sense that the environment responds to how people actually live — without requiring residents or guests to think about it, manage it, or call a technician when something changes.

Zakey's ecosystem was built for the MENA market and for the specific demands of premium real estate, hospitality, and commercial developments in the region. We design systems that integrate with the architectural vocabulary of each project, hardware that matches the finish quality of the spaces it enters, and scenes that are programmed for the cultural rhythms of the homes and hotels they serve.

Today, Zakey systems are installed across five countries and growing. Our partner network spans system integrators, interior designers, real estate developers, and hospitality groups who choose Zakey not because it is the cheapest solution, but because it is the one that makes their clients come back.

What Drives Us

Mission

To make premium smart-living accessible to every project that deserves it — by building hardware and software that integrators trust, clients love, and spaces come alive in.

Vision

A world where every villa, hotel, office, and home in the region feels as intelligent as it looks — and where automation is the invisible thread that holds the best spaces together.

Our Values

Premium First

Every component, interface, and interaction is held to a premium standard — because the spaces Zakey enters demand nothing less.

Integrator-Friendly

We design for the professionals who install and maintain our systems — with clear documentation, reliable APIs, and a partner programme that rewards expertise.

Privacy by Design

Smart-home data stays in the home. Zakey does not sell user data, and our systems are architected so that sensitive presence and behaviour data never leaves the local network without explicit permission.

Always Reliable

A smart home that fails at 2 a.m. is worse than a dumb one. Zakey systems operate locally, so power-cuts and internet outages never shut down core functionality.

Built to Scale

From a single apartment to a 300-key hotel, Zakey's architecture scales without additional engineering overhead — making rollouts predictable for developers and operators.

Why Zakey

Project-Ready Out of the Box

Every Zakey device ships pre-configured for rapid integration into a project environment — no factory calibration required on-site.

Designed for the Region

Zakey systems are engineered for MENA climates, power profiles, and cultural usage patterns — including Ramadan scenes, prayer-time notifications, and local language defaults.

Partner Network You Can Trust

Our certified integrators are trained, regularly assessed, and backed by Zakey's direct engineering support — so every project gets the same standard regardless of location.

Proven Energy Savings

Presence-based climate, scheduled lighting, and after-hours automation consistently deliver 20–35% energy savings across installed projects — with monitoring dashboards to prove it.

Lifetime Firmware Updates

Every Zakey device receives security patches and feature updates for life — so your system improves with time rather than becoming obsolete after a few years.

24-Month Hardware Warranty

All Zakey hardware is covered by a 24-month replacement warranty, with expedited parts fulfilment for hospitality and commercial clients to minimise any service interruption.

Global-Ready, Locally Supported

Zakey is built for the world, but delivered with local depth. Our partner network spans the Middle East, North Africa, and Southeast Asia, with certified integrators in every major market who carry local stock, speak the local language, and know the local building codes.

Every global market has its own timeline pressures, regulatory landscape, and client expectations. Zakey's architecture accommodates this: our systems meet international safety standards, support region-specific power configurations, and can be deployed across borders without reengineering the core solution.

For developers and hotel operators working across multiple geographies, Zakey offers centralised management, remote commissioning, and a consistent platform specification that holds from city to city — so scaling doesn't mean starting over.
