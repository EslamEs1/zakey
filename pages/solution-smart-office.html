Smart Office Solutions

The office that adapts to every meeting, every mood, every hour.

Zakey orchestrates climate zones, occupancy sensors, and scene presets across your entire floor plate — so teams arrive to a space that is already at the right temperature and the right brightness for the work ahead. When the last person leaves, the building handles the rest.

The challenge

Why modern workspaces need intelligence, not timers

  1. HVAC runs on timers regardless of occupancy

    Air conditioning and heating follow fixed schedules that ignore whether the floor is packed or empty — heating empty open-plan floors on public holidays and wasting significant energy budgets.

  2. Lighting is too bright for video calls, too dim for focused work

    A single ceiling brightness setting cannot serve every workmode — harsh overhead light creates glare on screens during calls and insufficient task illumination for detailed analytical work.

  3. After-hours energy consumption has no automatic cutoff

    Equipment left on overnight — monitors, HVAC, plug sockets — accounts for a disproportionate share of commercial energy costs with no mechanism to identify or act on it.

  4. Meeting-room availability is invisible to the floor

    Teams waste time walking the floor to find a free room, and booked rooms frequently sit empty because the occupancy system has no way to release no-show reservations automatically.

The Zakey answer

A workspace that reads the room — literally

The Zakey office stack begins with the Climate Hub managing HVAC zones dynamically — the Sense Engine's occupancy and CO₂ sensors feed real-time presence data, so ventilation and cooling ramp up when teams arrive and taper off as they leave. The Aura Panel on every meeting-room wall lets any team member switch between Focus, Presentation, and Video Call scenes in a single touch.

The Smart Plug layer ties the energy picture together: every desk cluster and breakout zone can be power-cycled automatically after-hours based on a schedule defined in the Zakey app. The result is a building operations manager's dashboard that shows live consumption by zone, live CO₂ per room, and a seven-day trend — all from the same app that runs the meeting-room scenes.

What's inside

Systems included in Zakey Smart Office

  • Central Control

    Facilities managers view live zone status, energy consumption, and air-quality readings for the entire floor plate from a single operations dashboard.

  • Lighting Scenes

    One-touch scene presets — Focus, Video Call, Presentation, After Hours — optimise brightness and colour temperature for every type of workspace activity.

  • Climate & Air Quality

    The Climate Hub manages multi-zone HVAC based on live occupancy data, while CO₂ sensors trigger fresh-air ventilation cycles before discomfort affects concentration.

  • Occupancy Sensors

    Passive infrared and desk-level presence sensors provide room-by-room occupancy data that drives HVAC, lighting, and meeting-room release decisions in real time.

  • Energy Monitoring

    Smart Plugs and panel-level energy meters capture consumption by zone, feeding live dashboards and weekly reports that identify waste and track against targets.

  • App & Voice Control

    Any team member can trigger a scene, check a room's CO₂ level, or adjust the thermostat from the Zakey app — without needing to touch a panel or call facilities.

  • Access Control

    Smart door readers and PIN-based entry points manage who can access server rooms, executive floors, and secure areas — with a full access log available in the dashboard.

Day in the life

Automation that keeps pace with your working day

  • Meeting Mode

    When a team member activates "Video Call" on the Aura Panel in a meeting room

    Then overhead lighting dims to a soft, glare-free level, side fill lights brighten to eliminate facial shadows on camera, and the HVAC fan speed drops to its quietest mode so the microphone picks up voices, not air noise.

  • After-Hours Lockdown

    When the last occupancy sensor on a floor reports no presence for twenty minutes past the configured end-of-day time

    Then all desk-cluster smart plugs power down, HVAC steps to overnight setback temperature, lighting extinguishes zone by zone, and a summary energy-saved event is posted to the facilities dashboard for morning review.

  • Air-Quality Auto-Vent

    When a meeting-room CO₂ sensor rises above 900 ppm with the room marked as occupied

    Then the Climate Hub opens the fresh-air damper for that zone and increases ventilation fan speed, while the Aura Panel display quietly shows an air-quality indicator — alerting occupants without interrupting the meeting.
