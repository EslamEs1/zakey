Hospitality

Smart Hotel Automation

A seamless stay engineered from the moment the door opens.

Zakey orchestrates every guest room's lighting scenes, climate temperature, and welcome moments automatically — so your team focuses on service, not systems. Vacant rooms switch to energy-saver mode the instant a guest checks out, cutting consumption without any manual intervention.

Get a Quote Explore Products

The challenge

Why hospitality operations demand smarter infrastructure

  1. Guest comfort versus energy waste

    HVAC and lighting in vacant rooms run at full load around the clock, inflating utility bills without serving a single guest.

  2. Inconsistent check-in experience across room tiers

    Standard rooms and suites greet guests with the same generic defaults — missing a key opportunity to reinforce brand positioning at the moment of arrival.

  3. Maintenance teams lack real-time room status visibility

    Engineers walk floors to check whether rooms are occupied, clean, or flagging a fault — a slow process that delays response times and frustrates guests.

  4. Brand differentiation in an era of commoditised stays

    With online booking driven by price comparison, operators need a tangible, memorable in-room experience that guests mention in reviews and return for.

The Zakey answer

One platform that runs every room, at every tier

The Zakey hotel stack centres on the Aura Panel — a wall-mounted scene controller that replaces the cluttered bedside remote — paired with the Climate Hub for precision room temperature management. Smart Door Locks integrate check-in status directly with the room's scene engine, triggering a personalised welcome sequence the instant a keycard is validated.

Beneath every interaction sits the Sense Engine: occupancy and door sensors that detect vacancy within seconds and automatically drop climate and lighting to standby levels. The result is a property that feels premium to every guest and measurably efficient to every finance director — a balance that neither legacy BMS nor generic smart-home kits can achieve.

What's inside

Systems included in Zakey Smart Hotel

  • Central Control

    A unified operations dashboard lets hotel management monitor room status, energy consumption, and fault alerts across every floor from a single interface.

  • Lighting Scenes

    Pre-programmed scenes — welcome, relax, sleep, clean — adjust colour temperature and brightness automatically for each room tier and time of day.

  • Climate Control

    The Zakey Climate Hub learns each room's thermal profile and pre-conditions the space to the preferred temperature before the guest arrives, then throttles back during vacancy.

  • Security & Access

    Smart Door Locks synchronise with the property management system — granting access at check-in, locking out at checkout, and logging every entry event in real time.

  • Occupancy Sensors

    Passive infrared and door sensors detect guest presence within seconds, giving the central system accurate occupancy data to drive energy decisions and housekeeping routing.

  • App & Voice Control

    Guests control lighting, temperature, and do-not-disturb directly from the in-room Aura Panel or via the Zakey guest app — no proprietary remotes or confusing menus required.

  • Guest App

    A white-label mobile experience lets guests pre-set their preferred room conditions before arrival, request housekeeping, and control every in-room system from their own device.

Day in the life

Automation that works at every moment of the guest journey

  • Check-in Scene

    When a guest's keycard is validated at the door lock for the first time after check-in

    Then the room lights rise to a warm welcome scene, the climate steps to the pre-set comfort temperature, and a personalised greeting appears on the Aura Panel display — all before the guest reaches the bed.

  • Do-Not-Disturb Mode

    When a guest activates Do-Not-Disturb from the Aura Panel or the guest app

    Then the corridor DND indicator illuminates, housekeeping is notified to skip the room, and all door-notification sounds are silenced — ensuring an uninterrupted rest without a physical sign on the handle.

  • Vacancy Energy-Saver

    When occupancy sensors detect no motion for fifteen minutes after a guest's keycard leaves the lock

    Then the lighting switches off, the climate setpoint rises to standby level, and an energy-saved event is logged to the property dashboard — trimming hotel-wide consumption without affecting guest comfort.
