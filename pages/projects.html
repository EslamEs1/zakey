Villa

Luxury Smart Villa

Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Challenge
Eight-bedroom villa with no unified control system — 14 separate remotes for A/V, lighting, AC, and blinds.
Solution
Full Zakey ecosystem: 6 control panels, 48 smart switches, automated curtains, presence-based climate, and 12 bespoke scenes.
Results
Single-panel control throughout, 23% energy savings, and a guest experience that became the owner's competitive advantage.

Smart Hotel Guest Room
