Real Estate

Smart Compound Infrastructure

One ecosystem. Every villa. One network.

Zakey gives real estate developers a single smart-infrastructure layer that spans every unit in the compound — from the main gate to each villa's living room — making the development premium and future-proof from the first brick. Buyers move in to homes that are already intelligent, already connected, and already carrying the developer's brand story.

The challenge

Why compound developments demand a unified smart infrastructure

  1. Each villa uses a different, incompatible smart system

    When residents choose their own smart-home vendors, the compound fragments into a patchwork of incompatible apps, protocols, and support contracts — making compound-wide management impossible and eroding brand value.

  2. Shared amenities have no centralised management

    Pools, entrance gates, lobby lighting, and communal gardens are controlled manually or with legacy timers — meaning staff walk the perimeter to adjust settings rather than managing from a single dashboard.

  3. Energy reporting is per-unit and manual

    Community managers collect electricity data unit by unit from meter reads — a time-consuming process that delays billing, frustrates residents, and gives no compound-wide consumption picture for sustainability reporting.

  4. The developer has no smart-home brand story to sell

    Competing developments now feature smart infrastructure as a headline amenity — without a cohesive technology proposition, developers compete on price and floor area alone rather than lifestyle differentiation.

The Zakey answer

One infrastructure layer for the entire community

Zakey's compound stack is built on the Mesh Bridge — a scalable wireless backbone deployed across the compound during construction that every villa unit connects to automatically. Each home receives its own Aura Panel, Climate Hub, and Sense Engine, all pre-configured to the developer's brand scene palette. The Energy Meter at each unit reports consumption back to the community dashboard in real time, eliminating manual reads and enabling automated billing summaries.

Shared amenity zones — entrance gates, pool lighting, lobby climate — are controlled from the same compound-wide operations centre that residents use for their individual units. The multi-unit topology means that adding new villas to a future phase requires only connecting to the existing Mesh Bridge backbone, not laying new infrastructure. For developers, this is a single negotiation, a single integration, and a single smart-home brand story that sells from the first showroom visit.

What's inside

Systems included in Zakey Smart Compound

  • Central Control

    A compound-wide operations dashboard gives community managers visibility over every unit's status, shared amenity states, and compound-level energy consumption from one interface.

  • Lighting Control

    Whole-villa scene presets delivered via the Aura Panel, plus compound-wide amenity lighting managed on automated schedules — pathways, pools, and entrance gates included.

  • Climate Control

    Per-villa Climate Hub management that residents control individually, plus poolhouse and lobby HVAC scheduling managed by the community team from the central dashboard.

  • Security & Perimeter

    Smart cameras, motion sensors, and gate access at compound entry points provide a unified security layer that security staff monitor from the operations centre and residents can view from their phones.

  • Occupancy & Environment Sensors

    Per-unit and shared-amenity sensors track occupancy and environmental conditions — informing automated amenity lighting, pool heating schedules, and vacant-unit energy savers.

  • Energy Metering

    Smart meters at every villa and shared facility feed live consumption to the compound dashboard — enabling automated billing summaries and a compound-wide sustainability report each month.

  • Curtains & Shading

    Motorised blinds and curtain systems in each villa respond to time-of-day schedules and sun-angle data, reducing solar heat gain and enhancing resident privacy without manual adjustment.

  • App & Voice Control

    Residents manage their individual villa from the Zakey app while community managers control shared amenities from the same platform — one app ecosystem for the entire compound.

Day in the life

Automation that serves residents and managers alike

  • Resident Move-in

    When a new resident's profile is activated in the Zakey compound system by the community manager

    Then the villa's Aura Panel is personalised with the developer's default scene palette, the resident receives a welcome notification in the Zakey app with onboarding steps, and gate access credentials are provisioned — all before the resident sets foot in the property.

  • Shared-Amenity After Hours

    When the compound pool and fitness centre reach their configured closing time and occupancy sensors confirm no presence

    Then amenity lighting steps down to safety-level, HVAC in the poolhouse switches to overnight setback, perimeter cameras arm to heightened motion-alert sensitivity, and an all-clear event is logged to the community dashboard automatically.

  • Compound-wide Energy Report

    When the first of each month arrives

    Then the Zakey system compiles unit-by-unit consumption data from every Energy Meter, generates a compound summary with per-villa breakdowns and month-on-month trends, and delivers the report to the community manager's dashboard and inbox — ready for service-charge billing with no manual data collection required.
