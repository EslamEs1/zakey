Climate / HVAC

Zakey Climate Hub

Intelligent climate that breathes with your space.

The Climate Hub connects to any HVAC system and adds Zakey intelligence: real-time air-quality monitoring, scene-based climate scheduling, and multi-protocol control over Wi-Fi, Zigbee, Modbus RTU, and KNX. Compatible with over 200 HVAC brands, it turns any existing system into a smart-living asset.

Request a Quote Download Specs

What the Climate Hub is

The Zakey Climate Hub is a universal HVAC gateway and air-quality monitor engineered to bridge the gap between existing climate infrastructure and the Zakey smart-living ecosystem. It communicates natively over Modbus RTU, KNX TP, Wi-Fi, and Zigbee — speaking the language of your HVAC system without forcing a replacement. A built-in sensor array continuously measures PM2.5, CO₂, humidity, and VOCs, feeding that data into Zakey's scene engine to adjust ventilation automatically the moment air quality dips.

Designed for villas, hotels, offices, and high-performance spaces like gaming rooms and server rooms, the Climate Hub mounts on the wall or inside a cabinet and connects via a four-wire terminal to the HVAC unit. Once commissioned, it integrates with Zakey scenes so climate becomes a natural part of routines like arriving home, hosting a meeting, or winding down for sleep.

Built for premium spaces

  • Universal HVAC Compatibility

    Works with Daikin, Mitsubishi, Samsung, LG, and 200+ brands via Modbus RTU, KNX, and IR blaster.

  • Air-Quality Intelligence

    Monitors PM2.5, CO₂, humidity, and VOCs, adjusting ventilation automatically when levels rise.

  • Scene-Based Scheduling

    Link climate to any Zakey scene: 'Arrive home' sets temperature to your preferred comfort level before you step in.

  • Energy Optimisation

    Learning algorithm cuts average HVAC energy use by 18% by predicting occupancy and pre-conditioning rooms.

Scenarios it powers

  • Arriving home

    When Zakey detects the resident's phone approaching the geofence

    Then Climate Hub pre-conditions the home to the preferred temperature 10 minutes before arrival.

  • Poor air quality

    When the CO₂ sensor reads above 1000 ppm during a meeting

    Then Climate Hub increases fresh-air ventilation and notifies the host silently via app.

  • Night-time cool-down

    When the 'Sleep' scene activates at 22:30

    Then Climate Hub gradually reduces the set point by 2°C per hour until it reaches 19°C, improving sleep quality.
