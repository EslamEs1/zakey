Smart Switches

Zakey Smart Switch Series

Control every circuit with a touch of precision.

The Smart Switch Series replaces standard wall switches with capacitive glass panels that respond to a gentle touch. Available in 1, 2, and 3-gang configurations with dimming, scene triggers, and multi-tap shortcuts — the switches blend invisibly into any interior.

Request a Quote Download Specs

What the Smart Switch Series is

The Zakey Smart Switch Series is a family of capacitive wall switches engineered to replace any standard toggle or rocker switch without requiring new wiring. Each panel features a tempered-glass face with a backlit indicator ring, built-in Zigbee radio, and a phase-cut dimmer on every gang — giving you direct load control, dimming, and multi-tap scene triggers from a single surface.

Designed for premium residential and hospitality environments, the switch series fits neatly into US and EU back boxes and pairs seamlessly with the Zakey Aura Panel, Mesh Bridge, and app. Whether installed across a whole villa, hotel corridor, or single serviced apartment, the Smart Switch Series gives occupants precise, beautiful control over every circuit.

Built for premium spaces

  • Capacitive Touch

    Zero-force, backlit glass surface with a satisfying micro-haptic feedback on activation.

  • 1, 2 & 3 Gang Options

    Fits standard back boxes in US and EU form factors; replace existing switches with no new wiring.

  • Dimmer Integration

    Built-in phase-cut dimming for LED and filament loads up to 400 W per gang.

  • Scene & Multi-Tap Triggers

    Double-tap or long-press assigns complex Zakey scenes without needing the app.

Scenarios it powers

  • Entering the bedroom

    When the bedroom switch is tapped once

    Then Zakey sets the lights to 60% warm white and the climate to the preferred sleep temperature.

  • Reading mode

    When the bedside switch is double-tapped

    Then Zakey dims ceiling lights to 15% and activates the reading lamp at full brightness.

  • Party mode

    When three switches are long-pressed simultaneously

    Then Zakey sets all lights to the party colour scene and lowers the shading.

Technical specifications

Power
AC 110–220 V, 50/60 Hz; in-wall powered
Connectivity
Zigbee 3.0, Bluetooth LE 5.0 (for commissioning)
Compatibility
Zakey Aura Panel, Zakey Mesh Bridge, Matter hubs
Installation
In-wall, US/EU back box, no neutral required on 1-gang
Material
Tempered glass face, polycarbonate back box frame
Dimensions
86 × 86 × 32 mm (1-gang); 86 × 120 × 32 mm (2-gang); 86 × 146 × 32 mm (3-gang)
Supported spaces
Homes, villas, hotels, offices, serviced apartments
