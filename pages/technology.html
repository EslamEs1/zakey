The Intelligence Behind Zakey Smart Living

Six core pillars combine elegant hardware, smart automation, secure connectivity, and scalable deployment — built for villas, hotels, offices, and signature developments.

Zakey was engineered from first principles around one uncompromising goal: to make every space feel intuitively alive. Our hardware is designed to disappear into premium interiors while our software layer orchestrates lighting, climate, security, and energy as a single coherent intelligence — one that learns the rhythms of a space and adapts without being asked. The result is an ecosystem that elevates every room from functional to genuinely exceptional.

At the core of every Zakey installation is a layered architecture that merges robust wired infrastructure with the flexibility of wireless control. This hybrid approach means contractors can work with the realities of any build — new construction, renovation, or retrofit — without compromising reliability or performance. Installers choose the connectivity path; occupants simply experience a home or building that responds exactly as it should.

Zakey scales from a single luxury apartment to a multi-tower development with equal precision. Our deployment tools give system integrators, developers, and hotel operators the repeatability they need to maintain quality across every unit and every project. Pillar 01

Zakey Link Architecture

A hybrid wired and wireless smart-home fabric built to perform flawlessly in every environment, from intimate apartments to sprawling compounds.

Zakey Link Architecture combines the unwavering reliability of a wired backbone with the installation flexibility of wireless connectivity. For new builds, structured wiring delivers deterministic response times and immunity to radio interference — every switch, panel, and sensor behaves exactly as configured, every time. For retrofits and renovations, wireless nodes slot into existing spaces without disruption to finishes or structure. The two paths work together under a single centralized control hub, so every device — regardless of its connection type — participates in the same scenes, the same automations, and the same unified user experience. This architecture is proven across villas, apartments, hotels, offices, and large-scale residential compounds, scaling gracefully as each project grows.

  • Wired backbone delivers millisecond-level reliability and zero interference for mission-critical control
  • Wireless nodes enable clean retrofit installations with no structural disruption
  • Centralized Zakey Hub unifies all devices under one consistent control layer
  • Scales from single-room deployments to entire multi-building developments

Pillar 02

Zakey Scene Intelligence

AI-assisted automation that understands the natural flow of life and transforms it into scenes that feel like second nature.

Zakey Scene Intelligence moves beyond simple schedules to create a living layer of automation that anticipates how a space is actually used. Whether waking to a gentle fade of morning light with the Wake Up scene, securing the property with Away, winding down with Good Night, welcoming visitors under the Guest scene, or sharpening focus in a Meeting environment — every scenario is crafted as a coordinated state across lighting, climate, blinds, and audio. Behind each scene is a condition engine that reads time, occupancy sensors, weather data, and user-defined triggers to activate the right environment at precisely the right moment. Integrators can build these scenes from templates and fine-tune them per client, while residents can personalise further from the Zakey app.

  • AI-assisted scene creation suggests optimal device states based on space usage patterns
  • Five ready-made scene presets: Wake Up, Away, Good Night, Guest, and Meeting
  • Condition-based triggers respond to time, occupancy, sensors, and location
  • Cross-device coordination ensures lighting, climate, and blinds always act as one
  • Scheduled and recurring scenes run automatically without any manual input

Pillar 03

Multi-Protocol Compatibility

One intelligent hub that speaks every language — bridging the world's leading connectivity standards into a single coherent ecosystem.

The Zakey hub works with Wi-Fi, Zigbee 3.0, Bluetooth LE, and is compatible with Matter-ready devices, while also supporting IR command output and wired control for legacy and specialist hardware. This breadth of protocol support means integrators are never boxed into a single supplier or technology path. Existing devices that support any of these standards can join a Zakey ecosystem without replacement. New installations can choose the best-fit connectivity for each device category. The hub bridges communication between protocols transparently, so a Zigbee 3.0 sensor can trigger an action on a Wi-Fi climate unit without any custom scripting from the installer.

  • Multi-protocol bridging connects Wi-Fi, Zigbee 3.0, Bluetooth LE, IR, and wired control in one hub
  • No ecosystem lock-in — works with devices from a wide range of manufacturers
  • Backward compatible with existing hardware to protect client investments
  • Future-ready with support for Matter-compatible devices as the standard matures

Pillar 04

Secure Smart Living

Privacy-first design and encrypted communications that keep every connected space — and every person in it — genuinely protected.

Security in a connected home is only as strong as its weakest link. Zakey addresses this by building encryption-ready messaging into every layer of device communication, so commands and sensor data travel protected at every hop. Device pairing uses a secure handshake process that prevents unauthorised nodes from joining the network. Project control is structured around a role-based access model — owners hold full administrative rights, family members receive customised access levels, and property staff or housekeeping can operate only the spaces and functions relevant to their role. Data handling follows a privacy-first approach, with user information processed locally where possible and never shared beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

  • Encrypted device communication protects every command and sensor reading in transit
  • Secure pairing handshake prevents unauthorised devices from joining the network
  • Role-based access control distinguishes owner, family, and staff permission levels
  • Privacy-first data handling minimises data collection and prioritises local processing

Pillar 05

Energy Intelligence

Real-time consumption visibility and automated optimisation that reduce waste without reducing comfort.

Zakey Energy Intelligence gives homeowners, property managers, and facility teams a clear and actionable view of how energy is being used across every circuit. Lighting loads, climate systems, smart plugs, and metered circuits feed consumption data into a unified dashboard that updates in real time. Automated optimisation schedules turn off idle devices, adjust climate setpoints during unoccupied periods, and dim lighting when natural light is sufficient — all without requiring manual input. Anomaly detection flags unusual consumption patterns, such as an HVAC unit running continuously or a circuit drawing unexpected load, enabling rapid investigation. Historical usage reports provide the data needed to track efficiency improvements over time and demonstrate measurable value to clients.

  • Real-time consumption monitoring across lighting, climate, plugs, and metered circuits
  • Automated optimisation schedules reduce idle consumption without impacting comfort
  • Anomaly detection flags unusual usage patterns for rapid investigation
  • Historical usage reports track efficiency improvements and support client reporting

Pillar 06

Scalable Project Deployment

Deployment tools built for professionals who need to deliver the same premium result across dozens — or hundreds — of units without reinventing the wheel each time.

Zakey gives system integrators, property developers, and hotel operators the infrastructure they need to move from a single showcase unit to a full-scale rollout with confidence. Room templates capture the exact configuration of each space type — bedroom, living area, conference room, guest suite — so that once a template is proven, it can be replicated across every equivalent room in the project with a single action. Repeatable scene libraries carry the same logic across units, ensuring every resident or guest receives an identical, polished experience from day one. Central remote monitoring gives operations teams a real-time view across all active projects, with the ability to push firmware updates, adjust scenes, and troubleshoot devices without an on-site visit. For portfolio operators managing multiple developments, a multi-project view consolidates status, alerts, and performance data in one place.

  • Reusable room templates capture proven configurations for rapid, consistent rollout
  • Repeatable scene libraries ensure every unit delivers the same premium experience
  • Central remote monitoring enables updates, adjustments, and diagnostics without site visits
  • Multi-project portfolio view consolidates status and alerts across all active developments
