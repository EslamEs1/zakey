Residential

Smart Apartment Living, Simplified

Complete home intelligence — fitted in a morning, felt every day.

The Zakey Smart Apartment package is purpose-built for urban living: compact hardware, tool-free installation, and a mobile-first experience that turns any apartment into a responsive, energy-aware home without landlord approval or structural changes.

Get a Quote Explore Products

The challenge

Smart living should not require a renovation

  1. Retrofitting is complex and disruptive

    Most smart-home systems require new wiring behind walls. In an apartment, that means landlord permission, contractor costs, and weeks of disruption most residents cannot afford.

  2. Energy bills are invisible until month end

    Residents have no visibility into what's drawing power — a forgotten air conditioner, a light left on all day, a charger running overnight. The bill arrives as a surprise.

  3. Security in shared buildings is limited

    Common entrances, thin doors, and shared corridors make apartment residents feel exposed. Traditional smart-lock systems are expensive and require professional installation.

  4. Multiple apps for multiple devices

    Without an integrated platform, residents juggle separate apps for the smart bulb, the thermostat, and the security camera — none of which talk to each other.

The Zakey answer

Smart living that starts in minutes

Zakey's Mini Hub sits discreetly on a shelf, connecting every Zakey device in the apartment into one coordinated network. The Edge Switch replaces any standard wall plate with no rewiring — just unscrew, click in, and reconnect. Add the Smart Plug for any appliance, and the Sense Engine motion sensor for lighting that turns itself off when you leave the room.

From the Zakey App, residents set "Morning" and "Evening" scenes that transition the whole apartment with one tap. The energy dashboard shows real-time consumption and suggests cutoffs. When paired with the Zakey Mini Lock, the front door reports its status and can be unlocked remotely — so the apartment is always exactly as secure as you need it to be, wherever you are.

What's inside

Six systems, one compact hub

  • Central Control

    The Mini Hub coordinates every Zakey device over a mesh network. No internet required for local control — everything responds in under 200 ms.

  • Lighting

    Scene-based lighting via the Edge Switch — dimming, colour temperature, and presence-triggered off. Fits any existing wall plate without rewiring.

  • Climate

    The Climate Hub connects to any split AC unit and controls it by schedule, occupancy, or scene — so the apartment is cool when you arrive and idle when you leave.

  • Security

    Smart door lock, entry sensor, and motion alert combine to give apartment residents real peace of mind — remotely lockable, with instant push notifications on any entry.

  • Energy Monitoring

    The Smart Plug tracks per-appliance consumption in real time. The app dashboard shows where energy goes and suggests auto-cutoffs for idle devices.

  • App & Voice

    The Zakey App is the control centre — set scenes, review energy, and check security status. Works with Google Assistant and Alexa without any additional hardware.

Day in the life

Routines that fit your day

  • Morning Routine

    When the morning alarm fires at 7:00 AM

    Then Zakey gradually raises the bedroom lights to 60 %, turns the kitchen light on at full brightness, and starts the AC on comfort mode so the apartment is warm and ready before you leave the bed

  • Evening Wind-Down

    When the "Evening" scene is activated from the app at 21:00

    Then Zakey dims every light to 30 %, switches the AC to a quieter fan speed, and locks the front door — creating a calm environment for the end of the day

  • Away Mode

    When the front door locks and no motion is detected for 10 minutes

    Then Zakey turns off all lights and non-essential plugs, sets the AC to standby, and sends a push notification confirming the apartment is secure and idle
