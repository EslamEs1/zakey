Security

Zakey Secure Kit

360-degree perimeter protection, intelligently managed.

A complete smart-security bundle engineered for discerning homeowners, villas, and boutique hotels. The Secure Kit combines indoor and outdoor cameras, a multi-point door sensor, a presence-aware motion detector, and a central security panel — all encrypted end-to-end and managed through the Zakey app.

Request a Quote Download Specs

What the Secure Kit is

The Zakey Secure Kit is a pre-engineered, all-in-one security bundle that removes the guesswork from protecting a premium property. Every component — the weatherproof outdoor cameras, the indoor motion detector, the multi-point door sensor, and the wall-mount central panel — is factory-paired, firmware-matched, and end-to-end encrypted before it ships. Setup is measured in hours, not days, and the Zakey app gives owners and facility managers a unified dashboard for live feeds, event history, and arming schedules.

The kit is equally at home securing the entrance of a private villa, the lobby corridor of a boutique hotel, the server room of a flagship office, or the perimeter gate of a gated compound. Deep integration with the Zakey Sense Engine means the Secure Kit is not merely reactive — it learns occupancy patterns, flags anomalies proactively, and coordinates with other Zakey devices so a security event can trigger a lighting response, an intercom call, or an emergency contact notification without any manual intervention.

Built for premium spaces

  • 24/7 Perimeter Watch

    HD outdoor cameras with night vision, weatherproof housing, and intelligent motion zones.

  • End-to-End Encryption

    All video, audio, and event data is AES-256 encrypted in transit and at rest.

  • Presence-Aware Arming

    Automatically arms the perimeter when Zakey detects everyone has left, and disarms silently on return.

  • IFTTT-Style Scene Triggers

    Connect security events (motion, door open, smoke alert) to any other Zakey device or scene.

Scenarios it powers

  • Leaving home

    When the last resident leaves and the geofence triggers

    Then Zakey arms all sensors, locks the door, and sends a confirmation to the app

  • Unexpected motion

    When outdoor motion is detected after 23:00

    Then Zakey turns on perimeter lights, sends a push notification, and starts a 30-second recording

  • Smoke alert

    When the smoke detector fires

    Then Zakey disarms the security mode, unlocks all exit doors, turns on full lights, and calls the emergency contact
