Quick Answers

How quickly will you respond to my quote request?

We respond to all quote requests within one business day. Complex or multi-site projects may take slightly longer as we prepare a thorough proposal — but we'll acknowledge receipt immediately and keep you informed.

What languages does Zakey support?

Our team works in Arabic and English. The Zakey app and control panels support multiple language settings and can be configured to match your guests' or residents' preferences.

What is the minimum project size Zakey works with?

We work with projects of all sizes — from single-apartment smart-home packages to multi-hundred-unit developer rollouts. For very small installations, we can often connect you with a trained local partner to ensure premium service delivery at every scale.

Do you offer on-site surveys before quoting?

Yes. For projects above a certain scope, we recommend an on-site survey to assess existing infrastructure, ceiling and wall access, and network topology. We can arrange this directly or through a certified Zakey partner in your region.

What after-sales support does Zakey provide?

All Zakey systems ship with a 24-month hardware warranty and lifetime firmware updates. Our support team is reachable by email, WhatsApp, and scheduled video call for technical queries. We also offer optional annual service contracts for hospitality and commercial clients.
