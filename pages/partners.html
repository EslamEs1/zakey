Common Questions

Partner Program Questions

Do you offer exclusive territory agreements?

We offer regional exclusivity agreements on a case-by-case basis, typically reserved for distributors with strong market presence and demonstrated project volumes. Territory discussions happen during the consultation phase. Exclusivity terms depend on the region, partner type, and agreed project commitments. Contact our partner team to explore whether exclusivity is available in your market.

What is the minimum order or project commitment?

There is no fixed minimum for system integrators and installers working on single projects. Distributors are asked to discuss regional stock commitments during consultation — these are tailored to your market size and projected volumes. We aim to make entry as straightforward as possible for new partners while ensuring stock availability.

How does the training program work?

Zakey offers a structured two-stage training program: online self-paced modules covering product specifications, installation standards, and app configuration; followed by on-site practical sessions for your installation team. Training is typically completed within two to three weeks of onboarding. Refresher sessions are available when new product lines launch.

What are typical lead times and stock availability?

Most core Zakey products are available from stock with 3–7 business day delivery within standard markets. Custom or large-volume project orders may require 2–4 weeks depending on configuration. Your account manager will provide project-specific lead time estimates during the quote stage.

Do you offer co-branded marketing support?

Yes. Active Zakey partners have access to co-branded marketing materials including digital brochures, social media assets, showroom signage, and project case study templates. We also support local marketing campaigns and trade show participation for established partners. Your account manager coordinates access to the marketing asset library.

How is partner pricing structured?

Partner pricing is based on your partner tier, order volume, and project type. Distributors and high-volume integrators receive enhanced margins. Pricing is discussed during the consultation and formally agreed in your partner agreement. We operate transparently — your margin on each project is clear from the outset.
